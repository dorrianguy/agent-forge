## ❓ FAQ Section

**Is there a free trial?**
We offer a 14-day free trial on all plans. No credit card required to start.

**Do I need to code?**
Nope. Agent Forge is 100% no-code. If you can fill out a form, you can build an AI agent.

**What AI models does it use?**
We support GPT-4o, Claude, Gemini, Llama, and more. Pro users can choose their preferred model.

**Can I use my own data?**
Absolutely. Upload PDFs, connect Google Docs, import from Notion, or point to any URL. Your data stays yours.

**Is my data secure?**
Yes. We encrypt everything at rest and in transit. Enterprise customers can self-host for complete data sovereignty.

**Can I white-label it?**
Agency plan and above includes full white-labeling — your brand, your domain, your clients.

**What happens when the AI can't answer?**
It gracefully escalates to a human. You set the rules for when and how escalation happens.
