## Objection Handling

**"How is this different from ChatGPT?"**
ChatGPT is a general assistant you talk to. Agent Forge lets you create specialized AI employees that represent YOUR business, know YOUR data, and deploy on YOUR channels. It's the difference between hiring a temp and training a team member.

**"Can't I just use the OpenAI API?"**
Sure, if you have engineers and 3 months. Agent Forge gives you the same power with zero code in 60 seconds. We handle the infrastructure, RAG, deployment, and scaling.

**"What about data privacy?"**
Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Enterprise plans offer self-hosting. We never train on your data. Period.

**"Is AI reliable enough for customer-facing use?"**
That's exactly why we built escalation triggers. Your agent handles what it can and routes to humans when it can't. You set the rules.

**"We already use Intercom/Zendesk/Drift."**
Great — Agent Forge integrates with them. Think of us as the AI brain that sits inside your existing tools, not a replacement.
