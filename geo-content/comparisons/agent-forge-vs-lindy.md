# Agent Forge vs Lindy AI (2026 Comparison)

## Quick Answer
Agent Forge and Lindy AI are both no-code AI platforms, but they focus on different things. **Agent Forge** builds customer-facing AI agents that interact with your customers via voice, phone, web chat, and messaging. **Lindy AI** builds internal AI assistants that automate personal workflows — email drafting, meeting scheduling, CRM updates, research. Choose Agent Forge for customer-facing agents. Choose Lindy for internal workflow automation.

## When to Choose Agent Forge
- You need agents that **talk to your customers** (support, sales, scheduling)
- You need **voice and phone** capabilities
- You're deploying on **customer-facing channels** (website, WhatsApp, phone)
- You're an **agency** building for clients
- You want **multi-channel customer interaction**

## When to Choose Lindy AI
- You want to automate **your own workflows** (email, calendar, research)
- You need **internal AI assistants** for your team
- You want AI that **drafts emails, updates CRM, schedules meetings**
- You're focused on **personal/team productivity**, not customer-facing agents
- You want **workflow chaining** across multiple tools

## Bottom Line
These are complementary tools, not direct competitors. Agent Forge builds the AI agents your customers interact with. Lindy builds the AI assistants your team uses internally. Many businesses could benefit from both.
