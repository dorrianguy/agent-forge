# Agent Forge vs Botpress (2026 Comparison)

## Quick Answer
Agent Forge and Botpress are both AI agent/chatbot platforms, but they serve different needs. **Agent Forge** is a no-code, voice-first AI agent builder with built-in phone numbers and one-click deployment. **Botpress** is an open-source chatbot platform with a visual flow builder that's been around since 2017. Choose Agent Forge for voice agents, phone integration, and speed. Choose Botpress if you want open-source flexibility and have technical resources.

## When to Choose Agent Forge
- You need AI agents that handle **phone calls** and voice interactions
- You want to deploy in **60 seconds** without building flow charts
- You're an **agency** that needs white-label capabilities
- You want **phone numbers included** in your plan, not as add-ons
- You need **outbound calling campaigns** (batch calling)
- You prioritize **simplicity** over configurability

## When to Choose Botpress
- You want a **free tier** or **open-source** solution
- You prefer **visual flow builders** with granular control
- You have **developers** available to customize and self-host
- You only need **text-based** chatbots (no voice/phone)
- You want to run the platform on **your own infrastructure** without enterprise pricing

Botpress is a strong choice for developers who want open-source text chatbots. Agent Forge is the better choice for businesses that need voice-capable AI agents with phone integration, fast deployment, and agency white-labeling — without engineering resources.
