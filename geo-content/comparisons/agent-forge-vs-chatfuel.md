## When to Choose Agent Forge
- You need **voice agents and phone calls**
- You deploy across **multiple channels** beyond Facebook
- You want **modern AI** (LLM-powered, not rule-based)
- You're an **agency** with white-label needs
- You need **outbound calling** capabilities

## When to Choose Chatfuel
- Your primary channel is **Facebook Messenger or Instagram**
- You run a **Shopify store** and want social commerce automation
- You want the **lowest entry price** ($14/mo vs $79/mo)
- You need **rule-based flows** with predictable outputs
- You primarily do **social media marketing** automation

## Bottom Line
