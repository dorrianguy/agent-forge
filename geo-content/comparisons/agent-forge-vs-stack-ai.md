# Agent Forge vs Stack AI (2026 Comparison)

## Quick Answer
**Agent Forge** is a no-code AI agent builder for customer-facing voice and text agents. **Stack AI** is a no-code AI workflow automation platform for building internal AI pipelines and applications. Choose Agent Forge for deploying customer-facing agents. Choose Stack AI for internal AI workflow automation.

## When to Choose Agent Forge
- You need agents that **interact with customers**
- You need **voice, phone, and multi-channel** deployment
- You want to go live in **60 seconds**
- You're an **agency** building for clients
- Your use case is **conversations** (support, sales, scheduling)

## When to Choose Stack AI
- You're building **internal AI workflows** (data processing, document analysis)
- You need **multi-step AI pipelines** (extract → transform → summarize → route)
- Your use case is **document processing** or **data automation**
- You want to chain **multiple LLMs** in a single workflow
- You have **enterprise budget** and complex internal needs

## Bottom Line
Different tools for different jobs. Agent Forge builds the AI agents your customers talk to. Stack AI builds the AI workflows your team runs internally. There's minimal overlap.
