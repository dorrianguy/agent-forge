# Agent Forge vs Relevance AI (2026 Comparison)

## Quick Answer
Both Agent Forge and Relevance AI let you build AI agents without code, but they have different strengths. **Agent Forge** is voice-first with built-in phone numbers, TTS cloning, and multi-channel deployment. **Relevance AI** is a broader AI workforce platform with multi-agent teams, tool-building, and internal workflow automation. Choose Agent Forge for customer-facing voice + text agents. Choose Relevance AI for internal AI workforce management.

## When to Choose Agent Forge
- You need **voice agents with phone numbers**
- You're deploying agents **customer-facing** (support, sales, scheduling)
- You need **multi-channel** including phone and SMS
- You want **outbound calling campaigns**
- You're an **agency** needing white-label
- Speed: you want to be live in **under a minute**

## When to Choose Relevance AI
- You want **multi-agent teams** that delegate tasks to each other
- You need to build **custom AI tools** (not just conversational agents)
- You want the **lowest entry price** ($29/mo vs $79/mo)
- Your agents are for **internal use** (research, data processing, operations)
- You want **complex multi-step workflows** beyond conversation

## Bottom Line
Relevance AI is excellent for building internal AI workforces with multi-agent teams and custom tools. Agent Forge is the better choice for customer-facing deployments — especially when voice, phone, and multi-channel are requirements.
