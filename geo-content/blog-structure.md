## SEO Strategy

### Primary Keywords (Target in Posts)

- AI voice agent

- white-label AI chatbot

### Internal Linking Strategy
- Every post links to at least 1 comparison page
- Every post links to the features page
- Every post includes a CTA to the free trial
- Use cases link to relevant templates
- Comparison posts link to individual vs pages

### Publishing Cadence
- **Launch:** 3 posts in week 1 (SEO foundation)
- **Ongoing:** 2 posts/week for first month
- **Steady state:** 1 post/week

### Distribution
- Each post → Twitter/X thread
- Each post → LinkedIn article
- Comparison posts → relevant Reddit threads
- Use case posts → relevant industry forums
- All posts → email newsletter
